About CampusConnect

CampusConnect is a smart and collaborative task management platform designed specifically for colleges and universities. It bridges the communication gap between Admins, Staff, and Students, streamlining everyday academic and administrative workflows.

With features like user authentication, role-based dashboards, and seamless task assignment, CampusConnect empowers staff to manage academic responsibilities efficiently, students to track tasks and progress, and administrators to oversee and coordinate various activities within the institution.

The goal of CampusConnect is to simplify communication, boost accountability, and enhance productivity across campus operations using a modern, web-based interface built with Django.
